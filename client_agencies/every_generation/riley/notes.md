# EG Model Family – Riley

**Age:** 18-19  
**Gender:** Male

## Demographics
- Gen Z male client
- Urban streetwear styling
- Fresh-faced, contemporary "lad" vibe
- Target: Gen Z demographic, streetwear/urban campaigns, youth marketing

## Appearance & Styling
- **Hair:** Gen Z "Broccoli Cut" / Zoomer Perm - sharp high skin fade with high-volume textured curly top (specific Gen Z styling)
- **Skin:** Smooth youthful complexion, absolutely no facial hair, fresh skin texture
- **Outfit:** Black technical puffer jacket (North Face style, matte finish, worn open), light grey branded t-shirt underneath, dark grey technical joggers/track pants, pristine white chunky sneakers
- **Expression:** Confident, contemporary, urban
- **Pose:** Standing straight, relaxed urban stance
- **Background:** Soft solid light grey studio backdrop

## Technical Specifications - 3x3 Grid Layout

### Row 1 (Top):
1. **Extreme Close-up** - Face only, showing fresh complexion and hair texture, neutral expression
2. **Medium Shot** - Waist up, hands at sides, showing jacket and layered outfit
3. **Wide Shot** - Full body from head to toe, standing straight

### Row 2 (Middle):
4. **Left Profile View** - Perfect 90° side angle, showing fade and curly top volume
5. **Three-quarter Front View** - 45° angle
6. **Three-quarter Rear View** - 135° back angle, showing back of jacket

### Row 3 (Bottom):
7. **Full Back View** - Rear angle showing back of jacket and joggers
8. **High-angle Top-down** - Camera looking DOWN at subject from above, subject looking UP at camera
9. **Close-up High-angle Selfie View** - Camera positioned above eye level looking down, head tilted slightly up, showing face and hair volume from flattering perspective

## Use Cases
- "Gen Z Style" campaign
- Urban/streetwear marketing

- Modern men's cuts and styling showcase

## Camera & Technical Notes
- **Style:** Ultra-realistic photography, 8k, Sony A7R IV, 85mm lens, sharp focus
- **Lighting:** Flat and even across all frames (neutral studio lighting)
- **Critical:** "Broccoli Cut" hair volume and curly texture must be IDENTICAL in every panel. This is the defining Gen Z styling feature.
- **Grid:** No borders between images, seamless arrangement
- **Consistency:** Frame 9 MUST be a flattering high-angle shot looking down at face
- **Emphasis:** High-volume curly hair, fresh-faced "lad" vibe, no facial hair
