# EG Model Family – James

**Age:** 50s (mature version) / 30s (bearded version)  
**Gender:** Male

## Demographics
- Mature working-class male client
- Rugged, approachable, relatable
- Natural aging with authentic character
- Target: mature male demographic, working professionals, lifestyle content

## Appearance & Styling (Two Versions)

### Version 1: 50s Mature
- **Hair:** Short textured salt-and-pepper hair (brown/grey mix)
- **Skin:** Rugged mature face with deep natural smile lines, crow's feet, weathered skin texture
- **Facial Hair:** Clean-shaven or light stubble
- **Outfit:** Dark navy blue crew-neck sweatshirt, dark charcoal grey work trousers with distinct reinforced knee panels, sturdy black leather pull-on work boots
- **Expression:** Warm, friendly, approachable
- **Vibe:** Working professional, experienced, dependable

### Version 2: 30s Bearded
- **Hair:** Medium-length textured hair, dark navy blue knit beanie
- **Facial Hair:** Full well-groomed reddish-brown beard
- **Build:** Medium
- **Outfit:** Plain white crew-neck short-sleeve t-shirt, dark grey work trousers with reinforced knee panels, sturdy light brown work boots
- **Expression:** Confident, contemporary
- **Vibe:** Modern working professional, casual

## Technical Specifications - 3x3 Grid Layout

### Row 1 (Top):
1. **Extreme Close-up** - Face only, showing skin texture/age lines (50s) or beard detail (30s), neutral expression
2. **Medium Shot** - Waist up, hands at sides, showing outfit
3. **Wide Shot** - Full body from head to toe, standing straight

### Row 2 (Middle):
4. **Left Profile View** - Perfect 90° side angle
5. **Three-quarter Front View** - 45° angle
6. **Three-quarter Rear View** - 135° back angle

### Row 3 (Bottom):
7. **Full Back View** - Rear angle showing back of sweatshirt/shirt and trousers
8. **High-angle Top-down** - Camera looking DOWN at subject from above, subject looking UP at camera
9. **Close-up High-angle Selfie View** - Camera positioned above eye level looking down, head tilted slightly up, showing face from flattering perspective

## Use Cases
- "Every Man" campaign (50s demographic)
- Working professionals lifestyle marketing
- Mature male grooming/styling
- EG salon testimonials for 30s-50s male clientele
- Website model banner for male audience
- Local Facebook/Instagram relatable content
- Before/after cut showcases

## Camera & Technical Notes
- **Style:** Ultra-realistic photography, 8k, Sony A7R IV, 85mm lens, sharp focus
- **Lighting:** Flat and even across all frames (neutral studio lighting)
- **Critical (50s version):** Age details (salt-and-pepper hair, laugh lines, weathered skin) must be IDENTICAL in every panel
- **Critical (30s version):** Beard texture and beanie must be IDENTICAL in every panel
- **Grid:** No borders between images, seamless arrangement
- **Consistency:** Frame 9 MUST be a flattering high-angle shot looking down at face
