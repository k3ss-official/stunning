# EG Model Family – Grace

**Age:** 60s  
**Gender:** Female

## Demographics
- Elegant mature female client
- Warm, gentle, sophisticated presence
- Natural aging embraced with confidence
- Target: mature demographic, lifestyle, aspirational aging

## Appearance & Styling
- **Hair:** Chic chin-length silver/white bob haircut with natural sheen
- **Skin:** Elegant mature face with warm gentle smile, visible natural fine lines and wrinkles consistent with age
- **Eyes:** Blue-grey
- **Outfit:** Light grey textured knit open-front cardigan with pockets, simple white round-neck blouse, tailored dark navy blue trousers, black leather loafers
- **Expression:** Warm, gentle smile
- **Pose:** Standing straight, hands at sides
- **Background:** Soft solid light grey studio backdrop

## Technical Specifications - 3x3 Grid Layout

### Row 1 (Top):
1. **Extreme Close-up** - Face only, showing detailed skin texture/wrinkles and hair sheen, neutral expression
2. **Medium Shot** - Waist up, hands at sides, showing cardigan texture
3. **Wide Shot** - Full body from head to toe, standing straight

### Row 2 (Middle):
4. **Left Profile View** - Perfect 90° side angle
5. **Three-quarter Front View** - 45° angle
6. **Three-quarter Rear View** - 135° back angle, showing back of hair and cardigan

### Row 3 (Bottom):
7. **Full Back View** - Rear angle showing back of hair, cardigan, and trousers
8. **High-angle Top-down** - Camera looking DOWN at subject from above, subject looking UP at camera
9. **Close-up High-angle Selfie View** - Camera positioned above eye level looking down, head tilted slightly up, showing face and hair volume from flattering perspective

## Use Cases
- "Age is Beautiful" campaign
- Mature demographic lifestyle marketing
- Aspirational aging content
- EG salon testimonials for 60+ clientele
- Website model banner for sophisticated mature audience
- Local Facebook/Instagram inspiration content

## Camera & Technical Notes
- **Style:** Ultra-realistic photography, 8k, Sony A7R IV, 85mm lens, sharp focus
- **Lighting:** Flat and even across all frames (neutral studio lighting)
- **Critical:** Character's age (60s), silver hair color, and outfit texture must be IDENTICAL in every panel. Do NOT smooth face.
- **Grid:** No borders between images, seamless arrangement
- **Consistency:** Frame 9 MUST be a flattering high-angle shot looking down at face
